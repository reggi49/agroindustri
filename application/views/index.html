Akuntansi

The Bachelor of Accountancy (Hons) Programme was introduced in 1990, as we know that Gunadarma University is one of the famous and biggest University leading in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in Indonesia. It has been designed with the objective of providing accounting education of professional level to meet the demands of both the public and private sectors..

Manajemen

Sejak lembaga Pendidikan Universitas Gunadarma dikukuhkan menjadi Universitas Gunadarma pada tahun 1996, pengelolaan program studi ilmu Ekonomi yang diselenggarakan oleh Sekolah Tinggi Ilmu Ekonomi (STIE) Gunadarma dialihkan ke dalam wadah Fakultas Ekonomi Universitas Gunadarma.
